The PAMO International school, Accra (PAMO) is a progressive public institution that provides both academic and professional higher education in Ghana. With over fifty (50) years of experience, PAMO has achieved the reputation as the oldest Ghanaian human resource development institution in professional Accountancy and Management with many of its products in key leadership positions in Ghana and abroad. PAMO remains the only public institution in Ghana with the mandate to offer both academic degrees and provide tuition for business professional qualifications.
